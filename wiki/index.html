DetecTME

Profiling the Tumor MicroEnvironment:

A synthetic biology tool to support cancer immunotherapy

Learn More

the rise of immunotherapy


Immunotherapy is a rising form of cancer treatment that activates the body’s own immune system to target and destroy tumors.

While extremely effective in some patients, only 20-40% of patients respond to immunotherapy.

tumor heterogeneity: a major problem within cancer therapy


Tumor heterogeneity一which refers to the diversity of tumor microenvironments一is one of the barriers limiting the efficacy of immunotherapies.

The clinical outcome of immunotherapy is highly dependent on the immune activity of the tumor microenvironment, but this factor is highly variable amongst patients.

predicting patient response to immunotherapy


There is an urgent need for a non-invasive way to better understand and characterize the immune activity of the tumor microenvironment, in order to predict patient suitability for immunotherapy.

With this information, clinicians would be able to prevent non-responder patients from undergoing the unfavorable side effects and financial burdens of an immunotherapy treatment that would not benefit their health.

Our Solution


We want to create a bacterial platform that can report on tumor immune status in-vivo, and generate a reporter that can be easily retrieved in blood or urine.
